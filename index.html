About

I am a dedicated and passionate graduate with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from NSBM Green University, Homagama. Throughout my academic career, I have cultivated a strong foundation in both the theoretical principles and practical applications of UI/UX design and UX engineering. This has enabled me to approach user-centered design with a deep understanding of how to create intuitive, efficient, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces.

In addition to my academic background, I have actively sought out opportunities to enhance my skills and knowledge. I completed a Web Designing program at the University of Moratuwa, which has given me valuable hands-on experience in designing user interfaces that balance functionality with creativity. Furthermore, my passion for emerging technologies led me to pursue an AI/ML course at SLIIT University, broadening my expertise and allowing me to explore how these cutting-edge technologies can be integrated into user experience design to create smarter, more adaptive systems.

I am continuously driven by a desire to learn and evolve in the field, and I look forward to applying my diverse skill set in UI/UX design, UX engineering, and AI/ML to create impactful digital solutions.
